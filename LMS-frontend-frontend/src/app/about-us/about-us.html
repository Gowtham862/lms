Al Bayan Learning is a dynamic, Abu Dhabi–based online learning platform dedicated to empowering individuals with industry-relevant skills. We combine expert-led training, flexible learning formats, and technology-driven education to help learners grow, adapt, and succeed in a fast-changing world.

Who We Are

Al Bayan Learning is an emerging and rapidly growing online learning platform headquartered in Abu Dhabi. We are committed to delivering high-quality, practical, and career-focused education through live online training programs led by experienced professionals.

Our platform is designed to support learners at every stage of their journey — from skill discovery and structured learning to assessment, certification, and career readiness.

Our Vision

To become a leading global online learning platform that empowers individuals with skills, confidence, and knowledge to thrive in a competitive and evolving professional landscape.

Our Mission

To deliver accessible, industry-aligned, and high-impact learning experiences through expert-led training, modern technology, and continuous innovation.
